Privacy Policy

Melissa Photography Paris

Last update: July 2025

1. Personal Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: name, surname, phone, email, address
  • Service Information: date, location, type of photo session
  • Payment Data: via PayPal (we do not store bank data)
  • Cookies: to enhance your experience on our website

2. Use of Data

Your data is used to:

  • Organize and carry out your photo sessions
  • Send galleries and deliverables
  • Manage invoicing and payments
  • Contact you about your booking
  • Improve our services

3. Image Rights

Important: Photos and videos taken may be used for promotional purposes (website, social media, portfolio) unless you explicitly request privacy when signing the contract.

4. Data Retention

  • Client data: kept for 3 years after the last session
  • Photos/videos: stored according to contract terms
  • Accounting data: kept for 10 years (legal obligation)

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You may:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify or delete it
  • Object to its processing
  • Request data portability

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Analyze traffic (Google Analytics)
  • Improve navigation
  • Remember your preferences

You can manage your preferences via the cookie banner.

7. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
